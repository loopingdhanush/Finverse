About us

The Finverse Club at PSG Tech empowers students with financial literacy, bridging engineering expertise with financial intelligence. Through workshops, seminars, and hands-on activities like investing simulations, members gain essential skills for managing personal and professional finances. The club fosters a community of continuous learning, preparing future leaders for financial independence and informed decision-making.
